Articulation Therapy

Does your child have difficulty producing certain sounds? Articulation therapy focuses on teaching children how to produce sounds correctly through playful and evidence-based exercises.

The Benefits

Clear Communication

Help your child be understood by family, peers, and teachers.

Boosting Confidence

Reducing frustration when speaking leads to higher self-esteem.

Our Process

We identify specific sound errors and their patterns using standardized tools.

Playful activities that focus on mouth positioning and sound repetition.
